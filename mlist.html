The mailing list is called mulinux-forum@lists.sourceforge.net. You may send messages to the list using the above address.
The main topic is muLinux, but we use the list also in order to discuss almost everything (Linux, computers, wines, etimology, regionals, ...)
Note: the language used on the list is the english language, but fractured english is allowed. Please, do not use italian or german :-)
